## Part 1: PMP (Project Management Professional) - Why It's Complicated

### Market Reality vs. Simple ROI

**Simple Version (What I Said):**
```
PMP: $5,555 cost, +$18,000/year salary premium, 4-6 month break-even
```

**Complex Reality (What I Should Have Emphasized):**
```
PMP gets you $18,000 salary premium ONLY if you:
1. Get promoted to Project Manager role (not automatic)
2. Are in industry that values PMP (some do, some don't)
3. Don't already have equivalent experience

If you DON'T get promoted:
  Your ROI drops to ~$2-5,000/year (not $18,000)
  Break-even becomes 2-3 years (not 4-6 months)
```

---


## Part 2: Lean Six Sigma - Why It Was Skipped

### What Is It?

**Definition:** Process improvement methodology + certification  
**Three Levels:**
- Yellow Belt: $1,000 cost, 1-2 week study, +$5-8k salary
- Green Belt: $2,500 cost, 2-3 month study, +$12-15k salary
- Black Belt: $5,000+ cost, 4-6 month study, +$18-25k salary

**Primary Industries:**
- Manufacturing (original/main use case)
- Operations/Supply Chain
- Quality Assurance
- Healthcare Administration
- Finance Operations

---

### Market Analysis: Size & Growth

**Market Size:**

```
Job market for Lean Six Sigma:
  Manufacturing jobs: 12 million (US)
  With LSS relevance: 4-5 million (35%)
  Active LSS job openings: 150,000-200,000
  
Comparison to other fields:
  AWS/Cloud jobs: 2.2 million openings
  CISSP/Cyber jobs: 1.8 million openings
  Data Science jobs: 800,000 openings
  Lean Six Sigma jobs: 150-200k openings
  
Ratio: LSS is 15-20x smaller market than cloud
```

**Growth Trends:**

```
2015-2020: Lean Six Sigma growing (manufacturing investment)
2020-2025: LSS declining (manufacturing automation, software focus)
2025+: Projected 3-5% annual decline

Comparison:
  AWS: +15% annual growth
  Kubernetes: +20% annual growth
  Lean Six Sigma: -2% annual decline (!)
```

**Why the Decline?**
- Manufacturing moving to automation (less process improvement needed)
- Tech companies dominating economy (don't use LSS framework)
- Agile/DevOps replacing Six Sigma in software context
- Younger workforce unfamiliar with methodology

---

### Salary Premium Analysis

**Green Belt (Most Common):**

| Source | Premium | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| PayScale | +$12,000-15,000 | Based on certified Green Belt salary survey |

**Black Belt (Less Common):**

- But: Requires Green Belt first
