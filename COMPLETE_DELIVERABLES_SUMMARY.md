### Q2: "What data science certs did you include? Why not CAP, SQL, etc?"
‚úÖ **DETAILED ANALYSIS DELIVERED:**
- `DATA_SCIENCE_CERT_SELECTION_EXPLAINED.md` - Complete breakdown of choices

**The 4 Data Science Certs We Included:**
1. **GCP Data Engineer** - $35,000 premium (ROI 13:1)
2. **AWS Analytics Specialty** - $32,000 premium (ROI 12.8:1)
3. **Databricks Certified Engineer** - $30,000 premium (ROI 12:1)
4. **Azure Data Engineer** - $28,000 premium (ROI 11.2:1)

**Why These 4?**
- Highest ROI (13:1, 12.8:1, 12:1, 11.2:1)
- Largest job markets (200-400k jobs each)
- Fastest growing (+18-36%/year)
- Verified salary premiums (4+ sources each)
- Directly applicable for military-to-civilian transition

**Why NOT CAP?**
- Niche market: Only 100k jobs (8-15x smaller than cloud data)
- Lower ROI: $10,000 premium (vs. $32-35k for our choices)
- Slow growth: +2-3%/year (vs. +36% for data engineering)
- Less relevant for IT background: Requires statistics, not IT skills
- **Verdict:** Didn't meet ROI cutoff. Better options existed.

**Why NOT SQL?**
- **It's a skill prerequisite, not a certification**
- Every data engineer knows SQL (table stakes, not premium)
- No standalone credential exists with verified premium
- All 4 data certs we chose require SQL anyway
- Including "SQL certification" would dilute actual career-boosting certs
- **Analogy:** Like saying "knowing Excel" is a credential for accountants‚Äîyou're expected to have it

**Why NOT Snowflake, Tableau, MongoDB, Cloudera?**
- All analyzed and ranked
- All fell below cutoff (lower ROI, smaller markets, or declining)
- Snowflake (best alternative): $24k premium, 70k jobs (growing +15%/year)
  - Would be Phase 2 option if market grows to 200k+ jobs
- Tableau/Power BI: Visualization tools, lower ROI than data engineering
- MongoDB: Niche (90k jobs), lower ROI ($14k vs. $32k for AWS)
- Cloudera: Declining market (-3%/year) as companies move to cloud
